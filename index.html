Timeline of Events

  • 32 BBY: The Phantom Menace – Anakin discovered by Jedi, Darth Maul defeated.
  • 22 BBY: Attack of the Clones – Clone Wars begin.
  • 19 BBY: Revenge of the Sith – Anakin becomes Darth Vader, Jedi Purge, Empire rises.
  • 0 BBY/ABY: A New Hope – Death Star destroyed, Luke joins the Rebellion.
  • 3 ABY: The Empire Strikes Back – Battle of Hoth, Vader reveals the truth to Luke.
  • 4 ABY: Return of the Jedi – Death Star II destroyed, Emperor defeated, Anakin redeemed.
  • 34 ABY: The Force Awakens – Starkiller Base destroyed, Han Solo dies.
  • 35 ABY: The Rise of Skywalker – Emperor returns, final victory for the Resistance.
